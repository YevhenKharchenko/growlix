How It Works

  • Match three or more colored tiles to clear the board.

  • Create powerful combos to defeat obstacles and villains.

  • Strategize your moves and use items wisely to complete tricky stages.

  • Explore over tons of levels that get trickier as you go

Characters

Meet the lovable bunny protagonist and its animal companions.

Every character's got their own personality and helps you out with special moves when you're playing. Unlock new allies as you progress, they all move differently and have their own little quirks. Characters become stronger with combos and milestone rewards.

Villains & Challenges

You'll run into these weird little villains that try to mess up your game.

Match strategically to overcome obstacles like lava rocks, beehives, and tough panels. Each villain introduces each bad guy throws different curveballs at you, so it doesn't get boring.

Events & Mini-Games

Participate in special events and seasonal challenges for bonus rewards.

Play mini-games for extra coins, boosters, and unique collectibles. The mini-games are pretty fun and help break up the regular puzzle gameplay.
